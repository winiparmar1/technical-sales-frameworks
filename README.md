## Philosophy

Most sales frameworks teach you to ask questions and listen. Challenger teaches you to **teach first, then listen**.

In cloud infra and SaaS, the buyer's biggest competitor isn't you — it's the status quo. Your job is to make the cost of inaction more visible than the risk of change. These frameworks are how I do that.
